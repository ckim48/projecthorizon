About

Decision Matrix Logic

Project Horizon provides an objective environment for community leaders and students to stress-test policy decisions against rapid technological change.

01. Ethical Evaluation

Analyze the moral implications of replacing human labor with automated systems in essential services.

02. Economic Projection

Use GPT-modeled data to forecast fluctuations in local economies and public welfare.

Motivation & Impact

Why Project Horizon was created and how it supports responsible thinking about AI-driven change.

MOTIVATION

Rapid advances in AI and automation raise complex ethical and economic questions. Horizon helps learners explore these issues through structured scenarios, rather than abstract or polarized debate.

IMPACT

By visualizing long-term consequences before real policies are enacted, the platform encourages clearer reasoning, informed discussion, and responsible perspectives on the societal impact of AI.

Features

How Project Horizon Works

CORE_FEATURE_01

Scenario-Based Decision Making

Step into realistic policy scenarios shaped by AI and automation. Users make difficult choices as community leaders, policymakers, or citizens—exploring trade-offs that affect real people and communities.

CORE_FEATURE_02

Impact Analysis Dashboard

After decisions are made, the dashboard visualizes how those choices affect jobs, economic stability, and social outcomes. Users can compare paths, explore trends, and understand long-term consequences at a glance.

CORE_FEATURE_03

Share & Reflect with Others

Share your reasoning, reflect on different outcomes, and discuss decisions with others. This space turns individual choices into collective insight and deeper understanding—supporting learning, dialogue, and responsible leadership.
