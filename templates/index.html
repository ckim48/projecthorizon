01. THE MISSION

Evaluating Ethics in the Automation Shift

Project Horizon provides an objective environment for community leaders and students to evaluate policy decisions against rapid technological change. Our logic matrix focuses on two critical pillars:

Ethical Evaluation

Analyzing the moral implications of replacing human labor with automated systems.

Economic Projection

Using GPT-modeled data to forecast fluctuations in local and regional economies.

Features

1

Scenario-Based Decision Making

Step into realistic policy scenarios shaped by AI and automation. Users make difficult choices as community leaders, policymakers, or citizens—exploring trade-offs that affect real people and communities.

2

Impact Analysis Dashboard

After decisions are made, the dashboard visualizes how those choices affect jobs, economic stability, and social outcomes. Users can compare paths, explore trends, and understand long-term consequences at a glance.

3

Share & Reflect with Others

Share your reasoning, reflect on different outcomes, and discuss decisions with others. This space turns individual choices into collective insight and deeper understanding—supporting learning and dialogue.
